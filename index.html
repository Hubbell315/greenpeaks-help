FAQ

  • Identify is taking too long: Try again on Wi-Fi or strong cellular, and use good lighting.
  • It says “Unknown plant”: Get closer, capture leaves + flower if possible, avoid dark shadows.
  • Login issues: Use Can’t login? on the Welcome screen to reset password or recover username.
  • Pro / purchases: Use Restore Purchases in MyAccount if Pro isn’t active.
  • Camera not working: Make sure camera permissions are enabled for GreenPeaks in your device settings.
